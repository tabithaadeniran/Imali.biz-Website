Core Innovation 🔗
Fractional Ownership & Tokenisation

Using Hedera (or Polygon/Avalanche), every stakeholder — landowner, marketer, lawyer, supplier — earns a % of the project through tokens. Making projects effectively "free" by removing mortgage dependency.

Our Philosophy

Data is our core business

Rwanda's biggest real estate problem isn't capital — it's fragmentation. Landowners don't know developers. Investors can't find vetted projects. Buyers lack neighbourhood intelligence. We fix fragmentation by being the data layer that connects everyone — and by earning only when our users earn.

No Subscriptions

We earn commission on transactions. If you don't earn, we don't earn.

Open Ecosystem

Not exclusive to one bank, one lawyer, or one developer. Anyone can join.

Youth-First

Lowered barriers, referral rewards, and education for young Rwandans.

Local Materials

Supporting Rwandan construction suppliers to reduce project costs for all.
