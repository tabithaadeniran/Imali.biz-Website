How to Invest

Four steps to your
first investment

From creating your account to receiving your first return — here's exactly how investing through the Wealth Hub works.

01
Getting Started
Create your account & verify

Sign up with your phone number or email. Complete KYC verification using your Rwandan National ID (NID) — verified against the NIDA database. Takes under 5 minutes. Once verified, your Investor Profile is live and you can browse all opportunities.

🪪 KYC via NIDA
Your NID is cross-checked with Rwanda's national identity database — no in-person queues.
📋 Investor Profile
Choose your investor type — Fractional, Full Asset, or both. Set your return target and preferred hold period.
02
Due Diligence
Browse, filter & evaluate

Every project on the Wealth Hub has passed Imali's six-category Development Readiness Assessment — scoring 75+ out of 100 before it appears. Browse by province, return target, project type, or hold period. Use our Returns Calculator to model scenarios. View each project's full Readiness Scorecard before committing.

📊 Readiness Scorecard
Legal integrity, physical viability, infrastructure access, urban alignment, and tokenisation readiness — all scored transparently.
🧮 Returns Calculator
Model your IRR, equity multiple, and year-by-year portfolio value for any stake size and hold period.
03
Committing
Invest via escrow

Choose your project and commit your stake. Funds are transferred into a dedicated escrow account held by a partner bank — Imali never holds investor money directly. Once the funding target is reached, tokens representing your ownership percentage are issued to your Imali wallet. For Full Asset deals, full title transfer is coordinated through our Compliance Hub with legal due diligence.

🔐 Bank Escrow
Funds are custodied by a regulated partner bank and released to developers only at verified project milestones.
🔗 Token Issuance
Fractional ownership is represented by blockchain tokens (Hedera Hashgraph). Your wallet shows your exact % stake.
04
Returns
Track milestones & earn

Monitor construction and development milestones from your portfolio dashboard. Dividends are distributed at each verified milestone completion — not just at project exit. At project sale or completion, your final payout is calculated against your token balance. For income-producing assets (rental), yield is paid quarterly from Year 1.

📈 Portfolio Dashboard
Real-time milestone tracking, token balance, dividend history, and portfolio valuation — all in one place.
💸 Milestone Payouts
Partial returns at each milestone — you don't wait until project completion to start earning.

FAQ
Common questions
What is the minimum investment?
Fractional investments start from RWF 75,000. Full Asset deals start from RWF 50,000,000 and above depending on the project. You can use our Returns Calculator to model any amount.
Is my investment legally protected in Rwanda?
Yes. All projects pass full legal due diligence via our Compliance Hub. Title is registered through NLA and the Land Administration Information System (LAIS). Tokenisation agreements are legally binding under Rwandan law, with marital consent where required.
What happens if a project is delayed or fails?
Funds are held in escrow and only released at verified milestones — so your money does not reach the developer until work is confirmed. If a project is cancelled, escrowed funds are returned proportionally. Imali maintains a legal dispute resolution process for all projects.
Can I sell my tokens if I don't want to invest anymore?
Yes. For fractional investments, a secondary market for token trades will be available. You can sell your ownership stake to another investor at the prevailing market price. Full Asset investors may negotiate a private sale through our platform.
How are dividends paid?
For income-producing assets (rental), yields are distributed quarterly from the date the asset becomes income-generating — typically from Year 2 for development projects. For capital appreciation deals, returns are distributed at milestone completions and final project sale.
Do I need to be a Rwandan citizen to invest?
Rwandan citizens and residents can invest immediately. Diaspora investors and non-residents are supported — contact our team for the documentation requirements specific to your residency status.
What is Imali's cut?
2.5% of the total return on completion. No upfront fees, no annual management fees, no hidden charges. We earn only when you earn.
