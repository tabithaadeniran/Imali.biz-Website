Two Ways to Invest

Pick your model.

Fractional Ownership 🔗
Own a slice.
Earn like a landlord.

Pool with other investors to co-own a verified development project. Each investor holds tokens representing their percentage stake and receives proportional returns at exit or through rental yield.

  • Start from RWF 500,000 — accessible to young professionals and first-time investors
  • Tokens on Hedera Hashgraph — transparent, auditable, legally backed
  • Automatic dividend distribution at milestone completions
  • Exit via token sale on secondary market or project completion payout
  • All projects are Imali-vetted and rated 75+ on our readiness scorecard
Minimum investment RWF 500,000
Browse Fractional Deals
Full Asset Acquisition 🏛️
Own everything.
Control it all.

Acquire full ownership of verified, project-ready land or completed assets. For high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and institutional investors looking for single-asset control with full title transfer.

  • Full title transfer with legal due diligence handled by our Compliance Hub
  • Imali arranges the full stakeholder stack — developers, suppliers, talent
  • Earn through capital appreciation, rental income, or project sale
  • GIS intelligence and market comparable analysis at every stage
  • Escrow through partner banks — Imali never holds your funds
Minimum investment RWF 50,000,000+

How It Works
