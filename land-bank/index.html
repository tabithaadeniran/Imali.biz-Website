Who This Is For

Built for two purposes

🏡 For Landowners
🏡
Own land in Rwanda?

Whether you have a small plot in Nyamirambo or a large agricultural parcel in the Eastern Province, the Land Bank gives you the tools to understand its value, list it properly, and connect it to the right buyers — all without paying anything upfront.

  • AI-powered instant valuation based on your land's actual details
  • Equity model: accept a % of project tokens instead of cash
  • NLA title verification and digital documentation
  • See buyer enquiries and negotiate digitally
  • Schedule physical viewings through the platform
  • Fraud protection and reporting on all interactions
List Your Land Free
🤝 For Agents
🤝
Help people sell their land?

The Land Bank gives agents a professional platform to list on behalf of clients, manage multiple listings, build a verified trust score, and earn commissions — without the friction of WhatsApp-only marketing.

  • List land on behalf of landowner clients
  • Manage a portfolio of multiple active listings
  • Build a public trust score through verified transactions
  • Earn commissions tracked and paid through the platform
  • Get matched to investors and developers looking for land
  • Partner Dashboard access: earn for every stakeholder you onboard

How It Works

From listing to sale

01
🪪
Register & Verify

Create your profile as a landowner or agent. KYC verification through NLA integration confirms your identity and land ownership documents.

02
📍
List Your Land

Enter your land details — location, size, type, and title information. Our team handles the technical assessment. Get an AI valuation estimate and choose your pricing or equity model. Publish in minutes.

03
🤝
Get Matched

Buyers, investors, and developers browse your listing. Our AI matches your land to the right stakeholders based on their goals and requirements.

04
💰
Sell & Earn

Negotiate digitally, sign via smart contract, and complete the transaction through escrow with banks as custodians. Earn cash or tokens — your choice.

Platform Features

Everything your
land deserves

We built the Land Bank specifically for Rwanda's land market — from title deed types to slope-based valuation adjustments. No generic templates borrowed from other markets.

AI Valuation ⚡
Instant Land Valuation

Input your land details and get an AI-powered value estimate immediately — accounting for location, gradient, title type, encumbrances, utilities access, and nearby hotspots.

Innovation 🔗
Landowner Equity Model

Don't want to sell outright? Contribute your land as equity in a development project and receive a percentage of tokens based on a transparent scoring system — title quality, slope, encumbrances.

GIS 🗺️
Map & Hotspot Intelligence

Every listing is geo-located and displayed on our multi-layer GIS map — with zoning overlays, flood zones, NLA registry data, and our proprietary investment hotspot layer.

Trust 🛡️
Trust Scores

Every landowner and agent has a visible trust score that grows through completed transactions and verified reviews. New users aren't locked out — they start fresh, not at zero.

Verification ✅
NLA Title Verification

Listings can be verified directly through our NLA API integration — confirming ownership, checking for encumbrances, and flagging title quality for buyers and investors.

Earnings 🌱
Partner Dashboard

Don't have land to sell but know people who do? Bring in landowners, agents, investors, or any stakeholder and earn commission for every successful onboarding you drive.
