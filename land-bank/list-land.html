Imali Development Readiness Assessment
After you publish, our team runs these checks. You'll see results in your dashboard.
◆ Legal & Title Integrity — UPI confirmed in LAIS, encumbrances, marital consent, succession checks
◆ Physical Viability — Slope gradient, flood & wetland risk, soil quality report
◆ Strategic Urban Alignment — Zoning codes, FAR/GFA density, road reserve setbacks
◆ Infrastructure Access — REG electricity, WASAC water, fibre optic proximity, road quality
◆ Commercial Viability — Market absorption rate, government reference price alignment
◆ Tokenisation Readiness — LAIS digital record confirmed, owner consent to tokenisation
